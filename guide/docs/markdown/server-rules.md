# Server Rules

## Overview

Welcome to the server! Please read all rules before playing. Repeated or serious violations
may result in bans, ship/station deletion, and penalties for everyone involved.

## General Conduct

- Be polite and respectful in chat.
- Do not spam chat or advertise other servers.
- Keep politics to an absolute minimum.
- Hate speech and hate symbols are not tolerated (racism, sexism, homophobia, etc.).
- Use common sense — if you are unsure about something, ask a staff member.

## Gameplay Rules

- Do not use cheats, glitches, or exploits. Report bugs to a staff member.
- Do not intentionally create server lag. Entities lagging the server may be deleted
  by staff without compensation.
- Do not create home-bases on planets.
- Do not attack or capture public infrastructure such as warpgates.

## Enforcement

Violations are handled at staff discretion. You can reach staff through the Discord server.
