# AtlasExchange

## Overview

AtlasExchange is a player-run marketplace where you can buy and sell ships,
stations, items, and weapons using Gold Bars.

## Opening the Exchange

Click the **EXCHANGE** button in the top-right player menu.

## Tabs

The Exchange has four tabs:

| Tab | Contents |
|---|---|
| **Ships** | Player-listed ship blueprints |
| **Stations** | Player-listed station blueprints |
| **Items** | Blocks and consumable items |
| **Weapons** | Ship weapon systems |

## Buying

### Buy as Blueprint

Clicking **BUY AS BLUEPRINT** on a ship or station listing gives you an empty
blueprint item. You must gather all required materials yourself before the
ship can be constructed at a shipyard.

### Buy as Design

Clicking **BUY AS DESIGN** on a *ship* listing (not stations) gives you a
shipyard design item. Load it into a shipyard's LOAD slot to begin manufacture
immediately — the shipyard will request the required blocks from your inventory.

### Buying Items

Clicking **BUY** on an item or weapon listing delivers it directly to your inventory.

## Selling

To list something for sale, open the Exchange and click **LIST ITEM** (or **LIST BLUEPRINT**
for ships/stations). Set your price in Gold Bars and confirm.

You can remove your own listings at any time by clicking **REMOVE** next to them.

## Gold Bars

All Exchange transactions use Gold Bars as currency.
Gold Bars can be obtained through gameplay or redeemed via AtlasBanking prize bars.

> **Note:** Blueprint purchases are server-validated. You cannot receive a pre-built
> ship from the Exchange — only blueprints or design items that still require materials.
