# AtlasExchange

## Overview

AtlasExchange is a marketplace where players buy and sell ships, stations,
items, and weapons using **Gold Bars** as currency.

## Opening the Exchange

Click the **EXCHANGE** button in the top-right player menu, or press the
**Open Exchange Menu** key (default **J**). You can rebind it under
*Options → Controls → MOD CONTROLS* in the in-game settings.

## Earning Gold Bars

You receive a **daily login reward of 1–3 Gold Bars** the first time you spawn
in each day. If your inventory is full, the bars are held and delivered once you
free a slot.

> A contract/mission system for earning Gold Bars is planned; the daily reward is
> the current way to earn them.

## Tabs

| Tab | Contents |
|---|---|
| **Ships** | Player-listed ship blueprints |
| **Stations** | Player-listed station blueprints |
| **Items** | Blocks and consumable items |
| **Weapons** | Ship weapon systems |

## Buying

### Buy as Blueprint

**BUY AS BLUEPRINT** on a ship or station listing gives you an empty blueprint
item. You must gather all required materials yourself before the ship can be
constructed at a shipyard.

### Buy as Design

**BUY AS DESIGN** on a *ship* listing (not stations) gives you a shipyard design
item. Load it into a shipyard's LOAD slot to begin manufacture immediately — the
shipyard will request the required blocks from your inventory.

### Buying Items

**BUY** on an item or weapon listing delivers it directly to your inventory.

Every purchase deducts the listing's Gold Bar price and pays the seller. If
delivery fails (for example, no free inventory slot) the purchase is cancelled
and your Gold Bars are refunded.

## Selling

To list something, open the Exchange and click **LIST ITEM** (or **LIST
BLUEPRINT** for ships/stations), set your price in Gold Bars, and confirm.
Listing requires being in a faction or having admin rights. You may remove your
own listings at any time with **REMOVE** (admins can remove any listing).

Whenever someone buys your listing you are paid in Gold Bars. If your inventory
is full at the time, the bars are held and delivered when space is available
(including across logins).

> **Note:** Purchases are fully server-validated. Prices, sellers, and items are
> taken from the server's own listing — never from the client — so you cannot
> receive free items or redirect payments. You only ever receive blueprints or
> design items that still require materials, never a pre-built ship.
